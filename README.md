# Project Overview: Evaluating Magist as a Strategic Partner for Eniac’s Expansion to Brazil
This project is a business-focused data analysis case study completed as part of the WBS Data Science Bootcamp. The goal is to help Eniac, a European e-commerce company selling Apple-compatible accessories, decide whether Magist, a Brazilian logistics and order management platform, is the right partner for expanding into the Brazilian market.

Eniac wants to scale quickly in Brazil but lacks local logistics, providers, and market knowledge. Magist offers an end-to-end solution—but concerns remain around product fit and delivery speed, both critical to Eniac’s brand and customer experience.

Based on 25 months of  Magist’s operational database, we determined if Magist is capable of handling high-end tech products and are reliable in terms of delivery of products.
