# 📊 Evaluating Magist as a Strategic Partner for Eniac’s Expansion to Brazil
🧩 Project Overview

# This project is a data-driven business intelligence case study completed as part of the WBS Data Science Bootcamp.

The objective was to help Eniac, a European e-commerce company specializing in Apple-compatible accessories, determine whether Magist, a Brazilian logistics and order management platform, is a suitable strategic partner for expansion into the Brazilian market.

Eniac aims to scale rapidly in Brazil but currently lacks:

Local logistics infrastructure

Established supplier relationships

In-depth market knowledge

Magist offers an end-to-end logistics and marketplace solution. However, key concerns needed to be addressed:

📦 Can Magist reliably handle high-end tech products?

🚚 Can Magist meet delivery speed expectations, which are critical to Eniac’s premium brand positioning?

Using 25 months of Magist’s operational database, this analysis evaluates:

Operational performance

Product-category compatibility

Delivery reliability

Revenue and pricing patterns

The goal: to determine whether Magist can support Eniac’s growth strategy without compromising customer experience.

# 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
MySQL Workbench – Exploratory Data Analysis

Used to query and analyze Magist’s operational database.

Core SQL keywords applied:
USE, SELECT, ROUND, AVG, COUNT, DISTINCT, AS, FROM, JOIN, ON, WHERE, IN, GROUP BY

Focus areas:

Order volume trends

Delivery time analysis

Revenue distribution

Category-level insights

Tableau – Explanatory Data Visualization

Used to communicate insights clearly and support strategic recommendations.

Visualizations included:

Pie charts

Bar charts

Geographic maps

Google Slides – Stakeholder Presentation

Used to structure findings into a clear, executive-level business narrative.

# 🔎 Key Findings & Strategic Insights

High-end tech categories show uneven price distribution and lower volume share.

Discounting strategies require caution due to potential margin compression.

Delivery performance is a critical success factor for maintaining brand positioning in Brazil.

📌 Final Recommendations

❌ Avoid aggressive discounting, particularly in categories with:

Large price dispersion

Low sales volume share

# 🧪 Conduct structured A/B testing to understand how discounts impact:

Sales volume

Revenue
